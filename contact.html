FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with our agency.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We respond within 24 hours for email inquiries and within minutes on WhatsApp during business hours (9 AM - 8 PM IST). For urgent matters, please call us directly at +91 9004329806.

What information should I include in my project description?

Please include: Your business type, project goals, target audience, specific features needed, any existing websites/designs, timeline expectations, and budget range if available. The more details you provide, the better we can understand your needs.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes, we offer a free 30-minute consultation to discuss your project requirements and provide initial recommendations. You can schedule one by filling out the contact form or messaging us on WhatsApp.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary based on project complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex web applications may take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline in your project proposal with clear milestones.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PHP, WordPress, Shopify, and more. We choose the best stack based on your project requirements and business goals.
