Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about working with CodersVerse

How long does a typical website project take?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A basic website takes 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms may take 6-10 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer transparent, project-based pricing. Costs depend on the scope, features, and complexity. Website projects start at $1,500, branding at $800, and marketing materials at $600. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer maintenance packages starting at $150/month, including updates, security monitoring, and minor content changes. All our projects come with 30 days of free post-launch support.

What information do you need to start my project?

We'll need to understand your business goals, target audience, design preferences, and functional requirements. During our consultation, we'll gather all necessary information to create a tailored solution.

Do you work with clients outside your local area?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. Our team is experienced in remote collaboration using video calls, project management tools, and regular communication to ensure seamless collaboration regardless of location.
